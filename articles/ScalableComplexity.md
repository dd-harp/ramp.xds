# Beyond Basic Setup

## Nimble Model Building & Scalable Complexity

**Nimble Model Building** and **Scalable complexity** are concepts that
guided development of **`ramp.xds`**.

#### Scalable Complexity

Motivating the concept of scalable complexity was that simple models
tend to work just fine most of the time, and there was sometimes a need
for models that were vastly more realistic. The point of **`ramp.xds`**
was to help users build models that were *fit for purpose,* but what is
the right level of complexity? A famous quote, that [maybe wasn’t
Einstein](https://quoteinvestigator.com/2011/05/13/einstein-simple/):

> *Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler.*

The quote conveys an important idea — no matter who said it — but it
does not offer any practical guidelines. How would you ever know without
building some models that have clearly gone too far?

Model building was thus designed to construct models with a high degree
of realism one step at a time.
